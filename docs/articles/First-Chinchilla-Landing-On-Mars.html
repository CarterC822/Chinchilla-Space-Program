Chinchillas Land On Mars after a 9 Month Journey
The Iris spacecraft landed on the Gale Crater on Mars on Apr 7, 2024. This was the first spacecraft to land on Mars with chinchillas on it. These brave chinchillas have gone through years of rigorous training to prepare for the rough conditions that outer space has to offer.
